How to play.

Find out how to play this game, the rules and keyboard shortcuts under this link.

Name of your game table:

This name (also called match ID) must be the same for all players who want to join the same game. Decide on a common and creative name for your game table. Players can rejoin at any time using this name.

Number of players (1-4):

Choose the total number of players in your game here. This number can no longer be changed after the game has started by any player.

Your name:

Pick a nickname for yourself.

Play as

Every player needs to play in a different seat, so discuss with the others who takes which number.

Advanced options:

  • flat field
    (pieces in your hand lie flat of standing)
  • show HUD
    (pieces in your hand are shown in 2-D in addition)
  • high-contrast shapes
    (uses an alternative set of shapes)
